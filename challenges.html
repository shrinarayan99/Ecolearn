Water Conservation Challenge

Take a 5-minute shower today instead of a bath to save water.

Why This Matters

A typical bath uses about 80 liters of water, while a 5-minute shower with a water-efficient showerhead uses only about 40 liters. That's a savings of 40 liters of water!

How to Complete

  • Time your shower to ensure it's 5 minutes or less
  • Turn off the water while soaping up
  • Use a water-efficient showerhead if possible
